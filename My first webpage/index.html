My Education

As a member of the life, safety, and audio-visual education committee in the class, I am enthusiastic, hardworking, and have a strong sense of responsibility in my work, laying a solid foundation for the study and life of my classmates. At the same time, actively participating in various campus activities, enhancing one's own abilities while possessing a strong sense of collective honor, and achieving excellent results in various competitions. In my spare time and during winter and summer vacations, I participated in rich social practices with my classmates to broaden our horizons; Participate in volunteer activities and establish a correct outlook on life and values; I am seeking an off campus internship, experiencing social labor, and understanding my parents and working people.

In life, I humbly pursue my studies, work hard and diligently, unite with my classmates, and am willing to help others. Three years of high school life have taken one's knowledge level, ideological level, work ability, and other aspects to a new level.
